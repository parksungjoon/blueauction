who we are

Since launching in 2006, Online Auction has been recognized as the go-to online auction marketplace for new, overstock, closeout and recertified products.

We specialize in offering the best deals on popular, everyday items from watches to laptops, and sports memorabilia from trusted and certified vendors and merchants, directly to our customers in auction, fixed-price and "Deals of the Day" formats.

what we offer

easy registration
To start using our auction, you’ll need to register. It’s completely free! After a few simple steps you will become a rightful member of Online Auction community.
Buy or Bid
You can instantly buy or place a bid on a desired product right after the registration. This allows you to use any auction or create your own in just a few clicks.
bid submission
Submitting a bid is quick and easy. The process takes up to 5 minutes and after that you get instant report from our online auction system about your bid’s status.
Win any lot
Easily win at our auction and enjoy owning the product you dream of. We will organize the delivery of the item you won so you don’t have to care about it.
free access
Unlike some online auctions, we don’t charge any additional fees for placing a bid and we provide our clients with completely free access to all auctions.
100% Secure Bids
We guarantee that every bid placed on our website is safe and secure thanks to the SSL technology, which protects all your entered data and actions performed on our website.
new Items everyday
We daily update our catalog of auctions with new offers from vendors who want to sell their products in a new revolutional way without any third parties and complexities.
24/7 online support
Our support team is always ready to help you solve any question connected with performance of our website. We can reoslve any issue efficiently 24/7.
