We're always looking
for talents

We work together to provide people all over the globe with first-class online auctions and opportunities to easily place bids and enjoy the desired products.

As a global organization, we operate in an environment without boundaries, creating a dynamic atmosphere of energy and excitement, career development and growth. Our colleagues are passionate about their work, stimulated by atmosphere around them.

corporate Culture

A good job isn't just about the paycheck and insurance benefits. Most people want to feel like their work is contributing to the greater good of the world. and that’s exactly what we give our team members at our company. At the end of the day, every employee feels that he/she is the part of a large family.

benefits

There are multiple benefits available to our employees, both experienced and new. Every employee has a challenging and fulfilling role in a great team, while dealing with great clients and rewarding projects. We provide our team members with free access to our social events and corporate benefits.

Learning

Working at Online Auction gives our employees a chance to challenge themselves and learn something new in auctions organization, while also communicating with important people in the industry. We also organize refresher courses that allow our junior employees to extend their skills and explore the world of online auctions without limits.
